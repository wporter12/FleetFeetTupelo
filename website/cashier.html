CASHIER TRAINING!

Transactions!

  1. Put in YOUR cashier number
  2. Put in the customers information
  3. Next, click ticket header
  4. Here you will scan all of the items. Make sure everything rings up correctly
  5. Proceed over to the Ticket Payment tab. Select the payment method from the list of options
  6. After Payment is complete, hand the customer their receipt and products!

Refunds!

  1. Put in YOUR cashier number
  2. Put in the customers information
  3. Next, click ticket header
  4. Change the transaction type at the top of the window to refund
  5. Scan the item or type in UPC and select whether the item is damaged or sellable
  6. Chech the receipt to make sure you are refunding the same amount the customer paid and change the saleperson to the cashier on the receipt
  7. Click Ticket Payment and choose the payment type the customer originally paid with. If they paid with a card, type in the transaction ID on the receipt and the funds will go back automatically

Giftcards!

  1. Put in YOUR cashier number
  2. Put in the customers information
  3. Next, click ticket header
  4. Change the transaction type at the top to Giftcard and enter in the amount the customer would like to put on the giftcard
  5. Pull a variety of different options out of the draw and let the customer pick one. Scan the card number on the back of the card to register the amount to that giftcard
  6. Proceed over to the Ticket Payment tab. Select the payment method from the list of options
  7. After Payment is complete, had the customer their receipt and giftcard!
